Welcome to Five Minute Farms!

Hello! I will show you how easy it is to grow your own veggies no matter what space you reside in. I will go over steps starting with the easy tomato and the herb Basil. Before long, you'll be making your own sauce! Lets get started.

Getting Started

Lets buy some simple ingredients. 1. A packet of tomato seeds. 2. Solo cups or styrofoam cups 3. Scissors 4. Potting soil 5. A bottle of water 6. A small LED lamp/grow light. 7. Cinnamon, small bottle, prevents mold

Easy Instructions

Cut the solo cup or styrofoam cups in half. Fill the half with potting soil. Use your index finger to push an indention into the middle of the soil, about 1 inch. Open the seeds. Place 1-2 seeds into the hole you made with your finger. Cover with more potting soil. Water until soil is damp. NOTE: Sprinkle a small amount of cinnamon over top soil to prevent mildew and mold. Use a small LED lamp or grow light as the sun source, or set near a window that gets light. Sprouting will take about 2 weeks. Make sure to check every 2 days and water as needed.

This works for a variety of vegetable and herb seeds! Here what I've used: Cucumber, Green beans, Snap peas, Tomato, Okra, Zucchini, Yellow Squash, Mini Watermelon, Sweet and Purple Basil, Thyme, Italian and Greek Oregano, Rosemary and Sage.
